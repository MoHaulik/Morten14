About Morten Haulik

Morten Haulik is an award-winning spatial computing developer, founder, and keynote speaker based in Copenhagen. Over a decade ago, I left my Information Science studies at the University of Copenhagen to join the first Nordic XR studio full-time. Since then, I have facilitated over 100 industry workshops, shipped high-impact immersive products, and delivered keynotes to audiences of 2,000+ people.

I have had the honor of presenting spatial computing solutions at official European Union gatherings for members of the European Parliament and Commission, as well as for United Nations delegations, demonstrating the real-world impact of immersive technology.

In 2025, I launched NXT/WebXR. Today, it stands as one of the world's most prominent spatial computing platforms, serving as the definitive gateway for daily users worldwide to explore the spatial internet.

NXT/WebXR Platform

The front page of the spatial internet. Can discovering immersive content be as seamless as browsing the web? That is the challenge I solved with NXT/WebXR. Today, it stands as one of the world's leading spatial computing platforms. Every single day, users from all over the globe visit NXT/WebXR to browse through immersive experiences and games directly from their headsets.

MidwifeXR & Healthcare Tech

As co-founder and XR architect, I took the stage at IDA's Driving Health Tech conference to demonstrate how MidwifeXR solves critical challenges on maternity wards. As part of their official education, midwifery students at University College Copenhagen (KP) have now integrated our platform directly into their teaching.

Apple Vision Pro Analysis - Berlingske Podcast

I was invited into the studio at Berlingske to give my expert opinion on Apple's massive entry into spatial computing. I explain why the headset is not (yet) a consumer revolution on par with the first iPhone. However, it is the ultimate developer platform for creating the immersive content of the future.
